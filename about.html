Who is Funky House?



"Here at Funky House, sweet childhood treats are the highlight of our work — it's all about our sweet memories”.

Born from a deep-seated love of all things culinary, Alexandra Tanase founded Funky House to satisfy her passion and deliver memorable moments to the many loyal customers who have come to love her creations.

Alexandra opened Funky House in Pipera Tunari in February 2020 with the mission of serving top quality cookies, pastries and cakes. We serve homemade sweets with an emphasis on flavor and quality of ingredients. Everything is made fresh daily from scratch.

In 2022 we introduced our own candy bars in addition to our house-made pastries. We now offer full candy bars for all sorts of events (weddings, baptism, corporate events, etc.)

Please contact us. We would love to meet you and see how to sweeten your day!
